Features

Everything You Need to Learn Smarter

A comprehensive suite of tools designed for modern academic management.

Study Notes

Browse and download PDF study materials uploaded by teachers. Organized by class and subject.

Assignments

View assignments with countdown timers, submit files before deadlines, and track submission status.

Attendance Tracking

Interactive rings and charts, subject-wise breakdown, and detailed session timeline views.

Fee Management

View payment status, month-by-month fee history with receipts — all in one dashboard.

Doubt Section

Students can write their specific doubts at any time. Teachers review them in their portal and post replies that instantly appear for the student.

Class Announcements

Real-time notifications and updates from faculty. Stay informed about schedule changes, upcoming tests, and important class news.
